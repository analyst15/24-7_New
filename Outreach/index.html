24:7 Outreach

At 24:7 Outreach, we are highly aware of our environmental impacts.

24:7 Outreach is a non-profit organization with the aim of providing environmentally friendly and social impact initiatives that promote sustainability. We empower the next generation whose dreams and actions will transform the future of communities and the world.

24:7 Outreach prides itself as the voice of conservation by influencing change through; Policy influencing, Sharing information, Conservation programs. 24:7 Outreach engages itself in the following programs that aid in conservation.

Our Commitment to Diversity

24:7 Outreach embraces diversity and is committed to promoting an inclusive environment across all types of difference. We commit to engaging in respectful dialogue, courageous conversations and bold action in service of a community becoming its best.

Diversity includes differences of perspective and preference, sexual orientation, political affiliation, economic status, veteran status, national origin or culture, race, age, gender, gender identity, gender expression, marital status, language, religious or non-religious background; physical, mental and developmental ability; and all other visible and non-visible differences.

As a group, Arts & Entertainment has given us amazing opportunities. We give back to the community so that young people can realize their potential through talent; artistic and creative expression for a chance to a better future.

Mission & Vision

Vision: Nurturing talents through knowledge, skills and services to empower individuals and communities for sustainable development.

Mission: Promote access to education and health among the disadvantaged members of the community.

Values: 24:7 Outreach lives out the values of collaboration, inclusion, and integrity.

  • Collaboration: Nurturing mutually beneficial relationships.
  • Inclusion: Celebrating differences because they matter.
  • Integrity: Keeping our word through our actions, transparency and honesty.

    • Objectives

  • To empower youth, women and people with disabilities economically.
  • To formulate projects that benefit the community.
  • To co-operate and assist projects, undertake sustainable programs.
  • To organize and carry out charity work on the less fortunate people in our communities.
  • To advocate for environmental protection.
  • To educate the community on social economic and life skills activities.
  • To carry out income generating projects for the members.
  • To support the vulnerable and disadvantaged members of the community to access education.
  • To foster sustainability awareness amongst targeted communities.
  • To do all things incidental or conducive to the realization of any or all the aims and objectives.
  • Developing ways to understand, provide access to, and organize biological data about our rich biodiversity.
  • Enhancing livelihood by partnering with communities living with wildlife.
  • Conserving biodiversity. Learning exchanges with communities.

    • The organization prides itself as the voice of conservation by influencing change through arts, entertainment & culture;

  • Policy influencing Sharing information Conservation programs engages itself in programs that aid in conservation.
  • Advocating in sectors that involve wildlife, forests, land, and water forestry.
  • By actively engaging all the involved governments to ensure all laws and policies are implemented.
  • Wildlife conservation through partnerships to strengthen institutional arrangements, building community awareness and improving on benefit sharing.
  • Marine conservation by encouraging the coastal communities to take ownership of marine resources and managing them in a sustainable way.

    • 24:7 engages itself in several programs that help improve the livelihoods of the communities. We've built a community where everyone counts, where all children are cherished and protected, and where individual and family life is valued and supported by all.

    24:7 Outreach envisions a nation connected through vibrant communities and networks actively in pursuit of social justice and educational equity - a nation where all young people can freely create their own path to enduring success that honors their passions, ambitions, and full identities.

    Volunteers are the power and passion behind the work: 24:7 Outreach has volunteer opportunities for individuals, students, families, civic groups, corporations, communities, friends and creatives to make a lasting impact together.

    Creatives for Change: Our role as creatives is to tell stories that make a positive change. Creating bold campaigns that leave a lasting impact; Expanding our outreach while keeping our shared passion for sustainability and social impact a priority.

    We are honored to maintain and carry forward the cross-stakeholder approach established so many years ago. We are an agency with deep ties to the families we work with, local congregations of faith, school systems, government, corporations, and community organizations.
