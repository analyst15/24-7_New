Who We Are

Founded in 2013, 24:7 Group is a purpose-driven creative collective designing immersive experiences that inspire culture, strengthen communities, and advance sustainability.

We operate at the intersection of: Creative Digital Media • Experiential Marketing • Sustainability • ESG

We help organizations transform purpose into measurable, lasting impact through integrated experiences that connect brands, people, and planet.

We are a brand-first, purpose-led agency built on a simple belief: When you move people, you move business. From strategy and concept development to full-scale execution, we design end-to-end experiences that strengthen brand equity while delivering measurable social and environmental value.

Through 24:7 Outreach, we amplify youth voices and unlock creative pathways that position art, culture, and innovation as forces for positive change.

Recognized for advancing sustainability and social impact, we partner with visionary brands, institutions, and organizations to turn purpose into measurable action, with honors including Africa’s Best Sustainability & Social Impact Agency 2025 and Kenya’s Most Innovative Agency 2024

To craft experiences that spark action, elevate purpose-driven brands, and create enduring positive change for people and the planet.

To inspire a future where sustainability and social impact shape unforgettable experiences across Africa.
